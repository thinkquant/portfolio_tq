**Title**
Intelligent Investing Operations Copilot

**One-line summary**
An internal copilot for wealth operations that retrieves context, cites sources, and stays inside controlled boundaries.

---

## Problem

**Section title**
The context bottleneck

**Body**
Operations work slows down when staff have to reconstruct account state, policy context, and next allowed actions manually.

---

## Why it matters

**Section title**
Why it matters

**Body**
A useful copilot in a regulated context is not one that speaks confidently. It is one that stays grounded, constrained, and inspectable.

---

## How it works

**Section title**
Grounded response path

**Flow**

```text
Question
  -> retrieve account and policy context
  -> call internal tools
  -> build structured response
  -> cite sources
  -> suggest next action or escalate
```

---

## Controls

**Section title**
Operational guardrails

**Bullets**

- source-cited response
- restricted actions
- no uncontrolled advice
- escalation when policy certainty is weak
